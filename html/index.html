Introducing Explo

Social Media shows us what seems the best of the world. The best possible food, places, styles, and culture. For us, however, the best of the world seems out of our hands. We seem not to find the tastiest food or the most beautiful, hidden graffiti our favorite creators posted.

What if the whole world was a vast museum and you could find digital tours everywhere? However, instead of a simple audio recording without locational context, your favorite creators from Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, etc., created them.

Explo makes this possible by making every creator a digital tour guide.
